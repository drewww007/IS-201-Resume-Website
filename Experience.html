Experience

Life's greatest teacher.

As a missionary for two years, I developed exceptional communication skills, cultural sensitivity, and the ability to connect with people from diverse backgrounds. This experience allowed me to cultivate resilience, adaptability, and strong interpersonal relationships while working toward meaningful goals. Additionally, my year of door-to-door sales sharpened my negotiation and persuasion abilities, as well as my capacity to handle rejection and maintain a positive outlook. These roles honed my ability to navigate challenges and foster connections effectively.

In the realm of entrepreneurship, I served as an ambassador for the Entreprenuership club at BYU. I am proud to say I have worked on two AI-driven tech startups. As a franchise consultant for an AI project, I applied strategic insights to help optimize business processes and operations. In the second startup, I played a key role in developing a safe social media platform aimed at improving digital spaces. Both experiences required creative problem-solving, teamwork, and a commitment to forward-thinking solutions that address pressing societal needs.

As an intern for a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive global impact, I embraced the opportunity to work on projects aligned with meaningful change. My role involved designing initiatives to address societal challenges and collaborating with teams to maximize outreach and effectiveness. Mentorship was another key aspect of this experience, as I provided guidance and support to peers while fostering a culture of growth. These contributions reflect my dedication to purposeful work and my ability to create user-friendly, impactful solutions.
